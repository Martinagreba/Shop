Bike plans for employees.

Veloretti Electrics benefit both the environment and the health of your employees. Oh, and they look good too. Cost-free for the employer and outside of the WKR.

Service,maintenance and insurance

The lease includes an all-in service package. Throughout the entire 36 months, you are fully insured against damage, theft and maintenance. In addition, you always have access to roadside assistance.

Free of charge for employers

The bike plan is totally independent of the WKR and is free for you as an employer. The employee pays the lease costs and you, as the employer, decide whether you want to contribute to the costs.

Get in touch

Any questions?

What about the paperwork?+

The bicycle plan is set up so that there will be no administrative work on your part as the employer. You can use a free online platform that has been created by our leasing partner. This provides you with a simple overview of your business and your employees, allowing you to arrange everything. It also simplifies all the paperwork. Everything is organised with just a few clicks.

How does it work with an employee who retires early?+

If an employee retires early, there are flexible options available to handle the situation. The lease contract can be transferred to the employee, allowing them to continue the bike plan independently. Alternatively, the employer can decide to take over the remaining payments, ensuring a smooth transition without administrative burdens.

What does a Veloretti Bike Plan cost for an employer?+

The Veloretti Bike Plan is structured to be cost-effective for employers. The lease costs are deducted from the employee's gross salary, reducing the employer's payroll taxes. Additionally, administrative efforts are minimal, as the plan is managed through an online platform, making it a financially attractive option for businesses.

How is the insurance & maintenance arranged when leasing a Veloretti bike?+

Insurance and maintenance are fully included in the Veloretti Bike Plan, ensuring a worry-free experience for both employers and employees. The insurance covers theft and damage, while maintenance is taken care of through certified service partners. This guarantees that leased bikes remain in excellent condition without additional costs or complications.

What are the legal requirements for the Veloretti bicycle plan?+

The Veloretti Bike Plan complies with local tax regulations and employee benefit laws. Employers need to ensure that the lease deductions align with payroll guidelines. All agreements are handled digitally, ensuring transparency and compliance with legal requirements while simplifying the process for businesses.
